How Sessions Work

  • Classes meet once per week for 45 minutes.
  • Monthly tuition is due at the first lesson of each cycle.
  • We maintain small groups to personalise instruction.
  • Make-up lessons are offered when notified at least 24 hours ahead.

What to Bring

Swimmers need a suit, swim cap, towel, and water bottle. Goggles are optional for the first few lessons.

No floaties required—our instructors provide hands-on support to develop balance and safety skills.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can’t find an answer? Contact us and we’ll be happy to help.

When can I start?

You can begin as soon as you are ready—classes run year-round.

Do I need any special equipment?

Bring your swimwear and a swim cap. We take care of the rest.

Do you use floaties?

No floaties required. Our hands-on approach keeps swimmers secure while they build balance and comfort.

What ages do you teach?

We welcome swimmers from 2½ years old through adults.

Can parents watch lessons?

Yes! Guardians are welcome to observe from the deck seating or shaded viewing areas. We ask for quiet cheering to keep swimmers focused.

How do make-up classes work?

Notify us at least 24 hours before your lesson and we’ll arrange an alternate session within the same month when space is available.

Do you offer private lessons?

Yes, we schedule private coaching blocks for swimmers who prefer one-on-one instruction or are preparing for specific events. Contact us for availability.

Ready to reserve your spot?

Head to our registration page to share swimmer details, or call us with any questions before signing up.
