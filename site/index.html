Welcome to J3 Swim Fit

We are a learn-to-swim school located in the Shoppes of Maraval, Trinidad, led by certified swim teacher and level 2 coach Jennice Price. From 2½ year-olds to adults, we teach every swimmer to feel safe and comfortable in the water before advancing stroke work.

Students progress from foundational water skills through pre-competitive technique for all four strokes, supported by a hands-on coaching team in and around the pool.

Quick facts

  • Small, convenient classes offered year-round
  • Hands-on guidance with teachers in the water or on deck
  • Programmes tailored to your pace and confidence level

Our Approach

Every lesson centres on water safety, balance, and comfort to build a strong foundation before introducing advanced skills. As swimmers grow, we emphasise stroke mechanics, breathing, and rhythm across freestyle, backstroke, breaststroke, and butterfly.

Our supportive environment keeps learning fun while moving at the swimmer’s pace, ensuring confidence in and out of the pool.

What you need

  • Swimwear and a swim cap
  • No floaties needed—our hands-on team keeps you supported
  • Just bring your enthusiasm to learn!

Frequently Asked Questions

When can I start?

You can begin as soon as you are ready—classes run year-round.

Do I need any special equipment?

Bring your swimwear and a swim cap. We take care of the rest.

Do you use floaties?

No floaties required. Our hands-on approach keeps swimmers secure while they build balance and comfort.

What ages do you teach?

We welcome swimmers from 2½ years old through adults.

Ready to Dive In?

Save your spot in the next session using our quick online form. A team member will follow up to confirm details and answer questions.
