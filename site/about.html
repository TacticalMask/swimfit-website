Our Story

Every swimmer deserves to feel safe, supported, and joyful in the water.

J3 Swim Fit was founded to create a welcoming home for beginners and returning swimmers alike. We operate out of the Shoppes of Maraval in Trinidad with a heated pool, easy parking, and flexible class times for busy families.

From the first splash to confident stroke technique, we tailor instruction to the pace and needs of each swimmer. Parents appreciate our clear communication while swimmers thrive with coaches who celebrate every milestone.

Meet Coach Jennice

Level 2 coach, certified swim teacher, and founder of J3 Swim Fit.

Coach Jennice Price brings over a decade of teaching experience and a passion for making aquatic skills accessible to everyone. As both a swim teacher and coach, she blends technical knowledge with empathetic instruction to help each student progress with confidence.

  • Certified under the American Swimming Coaches Association (ASCA)
  • Specialises in guiding nervous swimmers and learners who thrive with individualized support
  • Former competitive swimmer with national-level experience

Our Teaching Philosophy

We meet you where you are. Lessons emphasise safety and comfort first, followed by stroke fundamentals that scale with your goals—whether that’s family vacations, open-water events, or competitive aspirations.

Instructors remain close-by in the pool or on deck so swimmers always feel supported, and we communicate progress updates to guardians after every session.

What Families Appreciate

Comfort & Safety

Our first priority is helping swimmers feel calm and balanced in the water. We teach buoyancy, breath control, and safe entries before moving to more advanced skills.

Progress at Your Pace

Classes are capped so we can personalise drills for each swimmer. We adjust for sensory needs, schedule changes, and confidence levels.

Clear Communication

Guardians receive regular updates, tips for home practice, and reminders about upcoming sessions or special events.

Community

Students build friendships and cheer each other on. Our aqua fitness community often sticks around for coffee after class!

Facilities & Amenities

  • Heated pool with shallow and deep areas for progressive learning
  • Deck seating for guardians plus shaded viewing areas
  • Private changing rooms and hot showers available
  • On-site parking and nearby cafés at the Shoppes of Maraval

Partnerships

We collaborate with local schools and corporate wellness teams to provide custom programmes. Ask us about scheduling private clinics or team-building sessions tailored to your group.
