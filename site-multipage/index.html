Welcome to J3 Swim Fit

Led by certified swim teacher and level 2 coach Jennice Price, we help children and adults build a lifelong relationship with the water.

Whether your swimmer is just getting comfortable or refining technique, every lesson emphasises water safety, balance, and stroke mechanics. Our team works in the pool and on deck to keep learning supportive and fun.

  • Small, convenient classes running year-round
  • Programmes tailored to your pace and confidence level
  • Teachers in the water or on deck for hands-on guidance

Our Approach

Each class centres on water safety, comfort, and technique so swimmers grow confident in every environment.

“We teach every swimmer to feel safe and balanced in the water first, then build efficient stroke technique one skill at a time.”

  • Hands-on support from instructors in and out of the pool
  • Progress tracking from foundations to pre-competitive strokes
  • Celebrations of every milestone to keep motivation high
