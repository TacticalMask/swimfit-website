From the Pool Deck

Catch up on weekly highlights, tips for practice at home, and stories from our J3 Swim Fit family.

March 22, 2024

Saturday Splash Recap

Our Saturday morning classes were buzzing with energy this week. The 8:00 am crew worked through floating drills while balancing kickboards on their heads—an exercise that builds posture and core engagement.

Coach Jennice celebrated each swimmer as they held a relaxed back float for 10 seconds. Next week we’ll transition into rhythmic breathing drills so the group can add longer freestyle swims.

  • Milestone: three new swimmers floated independently
  • Focus skill: exhaling slowly through the nose
  • At-home tip: practice blowing bubbles in the bathtub
March 15, 2024

Aqua Fitness Playlist

Looking for a fun way to stay active? Our Thursday and Friday aqua fitness classes combine upbeat calypso rhythms with strength-building circuits. Coach Jennice’s latest playlist features Kes, Patrice Roberts, and Rupee to keep everyone moving.

We rotate through water jogging, noodle resistance training, and balance challenges on submerged steps. Each participant works at their own pace with modifications for joint sensitivity.

Download the 30-minute playlist and add light stretching before and after your session to stay limber between classes.

February 29, 2024

Meet Coach Maya

We’re thrilled to welcome Coach Maya to the J3 Swim Fit team! Maya swam competitively for Trinidad and Tobago and specialises in introducing breathing progressions to nervous beginners.

She will be leading Wednesday afternoon classes and supporting our adult sessions on Saturdays. Her favourite drill focuses on “float, breathe, reach” to keep movements fluid and relaxed.

Say hello when you see her on deck—she loves sharing stories about open-water races and can recommend great spots for snorkelling.

February 10, 2024

Upcoming Holiday Break

Please note there will be no classes from March 29 to April 2 while we refresh the pool deck for the new term. Sessions resume on Wednesday, April 3 with the regular timetable.

Swimmers can hold their spot by submitting April tuition before the break. Make-up lessons are available the week prior for anyone travelling.

Keep practising your kicks at home and tag us on Instagram with your dryland drills—we love to cheer you on!
